## Disclosure Best Practices

### Required Elements:

1. **Clear disclosure near the link:**
   - "(affiliate link)"
   - "(we may earn a commission)"
   - "(affiliate link - we may earn a commission if you sign up)"

2. **Maintain trust:**
   - "We only recommend tools we actually use and believe in"
   - "We receive a commission if you sign up, but this doesn't affect our recommendation"

3. **Optional: Site-wide disclosure:**
   - Add to footer: "Some links on this site are affiliate links"
   - Create a dedicated disclosure page
