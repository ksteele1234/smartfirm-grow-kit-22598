# Booking Automation for Bookkeepers

Booking automation for bookkeepers provides a self-service scheduling experience where clients and prospects book meetings directly based on your real-time availability. Instead of emails and phone calls to find a mutually available time, clients visit a booking page, see when you are free, and confirm a meeting in a single interaction. The system handles the rest: calendar confirmation, email notification, reminder delivery, and follow up after the meeting. For bookkeepers who manage many client relationships with limited administrative support, booking automation eliminates one of the most time-consuming non-bookkeeping tasks in the practice.

## The Scheduling Burden for Bookkeepers

Bookkeepers spend a disproportionate amount of time on meeting logistics relative to the actual meeting value.

**The back-and-forth problem.** A typical meeting request involves: you suggest times, the client says none of those work, you suggest more times, the client picks one, you send a calendar invitation, the client accepts. Five to ten messages for a single meeting. Across a month's worth of client meetings and prospect consultations, the scheduling emails alone can consume hours.

**The interruption cost.** Every scheduling email is an interruption to whatever you are working on. Checking your calendar, composing a response, and sending it takes less than a minute, but the context switch from bookkeeping work to email and back costs more than the email itself.

**The missed opportunity.** When a prospect visits your website and wants to schedule a consultation, they have to send an email or call and wait for a response. If the response takes hours or days (because you are doing client work), the prospect may have already contacted another bookkeeper. Self-service booking captures the prospect at the moment of interest.

**The no-show problem.** When meetings are scheduled informally (phone call agreement, email exchange without a formal calendar entry), the risk of forgetting increases for both parties. No-shows waste the time you set aside and disrupt your schedule for the rest of the day.

## What Booking Automation Provides

**A client-facing booking page.** A professional, branded page that shows your available time slots and lets clients book instantly. The page can be embedded on your website, linked from your email signature, or shared as a standalone URL. Clients see only the times you are available, eliminating the guesswork.

**Real-time availability.** The booking page connects to your calendar and reflects your current availability. If you add a meeting to your calendar, the booking page removes that time slot immediately. Availability is always accurate because it reads from your actual calendar, not a manually maintained list.

**Instant confirmation.** When a client books, they receive an immediate confirmation email with the meeting details: date, time, location or video link, and any instructions. You receive a notification and the meeting appears on your calendar. Both parties have clear confirmation within seconds.

**Automated reminders.** Reminders are sent at intervals you define: a day before, an hour before, or both. Reminders reduce no-shows and ensure clients arrive prepared. For meetings that require the client to bring or prepare something, the reminder includes specific instructions.

**Different meeting types.** Your booking page can offer different types of meetings: a 15-minute introductory call for prospects, a 30-minute quarterly review for clients, a 60-minute year-end planning session. Each type has its own duration, availability, and instructions.

**Post-meeting follow up.** After the meeting occurs, the system sends a follow up: a summary of action items, links to relevant resources, or a booking link for the next meeting. Follow up is automatic and tailored to the meeting type.

## Your Questions Answered

**What if a client books a time that I need to block for other work?**
The system only shows times that are available on your calendar. If you block time on your calendar for focused work, client deadlines, or personal commitments, those blocks are not shown on the booking page. You maintain full control over your availability.

**Can prospects book without being an existing client?**
Yes. You can create meeting types that are open to anyone (like an initial consultation) and meeting types that are restricted to existing clients. Prospect booking pages can be placed on your website to capture new inquiries.

**Does this handle virtual and in-person meetings?**
Yes. Each meeting type can be configured as virtual (with an automatic video call link), in-person (with a location address), or client's choice. The confirmation email includes the relevant details for whichever format applies.

**What if I need to reschedule a meeting I already confirmed?**
You can reschedule from within the system or directly on your calendar. The client receives an automatic notification about the change with a link to confirm the new time or choose a different one.
